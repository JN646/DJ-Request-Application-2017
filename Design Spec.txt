Software Design Specification.
******************************

This document has been created to document the design specification of the DJ Request system.

CONCEPT
The concept of the application is to provide a means for clients (primarily in private booths) to send requests straight to the DJ.

THE CLIENT
The role of the client is to send requests to the DJ by choosing songs from a song list.

THE DJ
The DJ willl receive songs sent by clients and then approve or reject them.
